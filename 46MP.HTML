What is AI?

AI is a computer program made to think and act like humans. Think of it this way, if you've watched Iron Man, then you must have heard of J.A.R.V.I.S. JARVIS is AI. It is used to make Iron Man's lide easier, just like how AI make our life easier.


Who made AI?

There is no one person who made AI. In fact, John McCarthy is one of the "founding fathers" of artificial intelligence, together with Alan Turing, Marvin Minsky, Allen Newell, and Herbert A. But these people are who many consider to be the makers of AI.
